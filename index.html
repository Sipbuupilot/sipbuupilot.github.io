    Reasons to donate.    

    Feeding the People in need.    

    Donating food can change a bunch of lives. Even by donating a few cans, you can change so many lives because you give them food that they most likely cannot get or afford. A quote from the Food Bank website, "Our duty is to serve hungry children, families, and seniors. Your generosity changes lives. "    

    Helping families that don't have diapers and etc.    

    Donating items such as diapers, dogfood, toys, and etc can help families that can't afford those items and really need it for their babies and dogs. Even donating a few of those items can really help people.
